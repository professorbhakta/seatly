# Product vision

## What Seatly is

Seatly is a **GSRTC-first bus awareness and ticketing companion** with two faces:

1. **Passenger** — search routes, see line-up, timings, and occupancy before boarding.
2. **Conductor ETM** — seat-map-first selling: tap a free seat, payment starts automatically.

## What Seatly is not (v0.1)

- Not a full RedBus-style intercity booking platform
- Not a city-bus pass/ticket app like Chalo (and we do not use the name Chalo)
- Not officially affiliated with GSRTC
- Not real-time GPS production tracking yet (demo map only with confirmed PNR)

## North star

Passengers **know before they board**. Conductors **sell from the seat map**, not from separate “mark filled” chores. Occupancy comes from ticket issuance: online bookings + onboard seated tickets + standing tickets.

## Expansion path (later)

GSRTC first. Other STUs and private operators only after the core flows are proven.
