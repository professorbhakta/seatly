# Competitive notes

## RedBus

- **Focus:** Intercity bus **booking** and discovery across operators.
- **Gap for Seatly:** RedBus is not built around GSRTC stand-level line-up, conductor ETM, or standing-passenger awareness at boarding time.
- **Seatly angle:** Awareness before board + conductor-side seat map, not primarily a booking marketplace.

## Chalo (name taken — do not use)

- **Focus:** City bus live tracking, digital tickets, passes (urban transit).
- **Gap for Seatly:** Chalo optimizes urban commute; Seatly targets GSRTC-style long/intercity route awareness and conductor ETM.
- **Naming:** “Chalo” is taken and would cause confusion. Seatly uses a distinct identity.

## Positioning summary

Seatly sits between **passenger awareness** (line-up, occupancy, conditional live track) and **conductor tooling** (modern ETM). It is not trying to replace RedBus booking or Chalo city passes in v0.1.
