# Origin & motivation

Seatly exists because GSRTC-style bus passengers often lack clear, timely information before they board.

## The everyday problem

People using state transport buses frequently cannot:

- see upcoming buses for their stand or a from→to route
- understand timing and line-up clearly
- know seat availability — or when a bus is full with standing passengers
- track a bus live without confusion or multiple apps

Current conductor Electronic Ticket Machine (ETM) interfaces feel dated and hard to use. Passengers are left guessing.

## Founder intent

The goal is **awareness and an easier process** for everyone — passenger and conductor — not “life-changing logistics” hype.

Seatly is GSRTC-first awareness plus a modern conductor ETM experience. It is a demo product today: sample Gujarat stations, simulated UPI, and no official GSRTC affiliation.

## Tagline direction

**Asaan sawari — know before you board.**
