# Naming

## Working name: Seatly

**Seatly** is the temporary product name. Tagline direction:

> **Asaan sawari — know before you board.**

## Why Seatly (for now)

- Short, memorable, seat-adjacent
- Room to grow into passenger + conductor product
- Not using “Chalo” (taken, different category)

## Caution

Other products use **Seatly** (restaurants, events) and **OpenSeat** (tickets, carpool). A future rename is possible. v0.1 should not block on final branding.
